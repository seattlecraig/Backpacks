=====================================
BACKPACKS GUIDE
=====================================
A player's guide to portable storage

By SupaFloof Games, LLC
=====================================
PAGE 1
=====================================
WHAT ARE BACKPACKS?

Backpacks are portable storage containers.

They hold items like a chest that you can carry and open anywhere.
=====================================
PAGE 2
=====================================
IDENTIFYING BACKPACKS

Backpacks have:
- Gold bold text name
- Enchanted glow effect
- Lore text showing capacity

Two types exist:
- 27 slots (standard)
- 54 slots (upgraded)
=====================================
PAGE 3
=====================================
OPENING A BACKPACK

1. Hold the backpack in your hand

2. Right-click

3. A storage GUI opens

4. Move items in and out

5. Close with ESC or inventory key
=====================================
PAGE 4
=====================================
WHEN YOU CLOSE

You will see:
"Backpack saved!"

Your items are saved immediately.

Each backpack stores its own items separately.
=====================================
PAGE 5
=====================================
STANDARD BACKPACK

Name: Backpack
Color: Gold
Capacity: 27 slots

Lore shows:
"A portable storage container"
"Capacity: 27 slots"
"Right-click to open"
=====================================
PAGE 6
=====================================
UPGRADED BACKPACK

Name: Backpack
Color: Gold
Capacity: 54 slots

Lore shows:
"A portable storage container"
"Capacity: 54 slots"
"✦ UPGRADED ✦"
"Right-click to open"
=====================================
PAGE 7
=====================================
CAPACITY DOUBLER

This is a paper item that upgrades backpacks.

Name: Backpack Capacity Doubler
Color: Aqua
Enchanted glow

Upgrades 27 slots to 54 slots
=====================================
PAGE 8
=====================================
HOW TO UPGRADE

1. Have a 27-slot backpack
2. Have a Capacity Doubler
3. Pick up the doubler
4. Right-click it onto the backpack

Works in any inventory.
=====================================
PAGE 9
=====================================
UPGRADING RULES

- Backpack must be alone (not stacked)
- Can only upgrade once
- Already 54-slot backpacks cannot be upgraded
- Doubler is consumed
=====================================
PAGE 10
=====================================
AFTER UPGRADING

You will see:
"✦ Backpack upgraded to 54 slots! ✦"

You will hear a level-up sound.

All items inside stay in place.
=====================================
PAGE 11
=====================================
BACKPACK STORAGE

Each backpack has a unique ID.

Your items are separate from other players.

Multiple backpacks each have their own storage.
=====================================
PAGE 12
=====================================
BACKPACKS IN BACKPACKS

You cannot put backpacks inside other backpacks.

If you try, you will see:
"You cannot put a backpack inside another backpack!"
=====================================
PAGE 13
=====================================
USING MULTIPLE BACKPACKS

You can own as many backpacks as you want.

Each one stores different items.

Organize them by purpose:
- Mining supplies
- Farming tools
- Building blocks
=====================================
PAGE 14
=====================================
QUICK REFERENCE

OPEN: Hold + Right-click

UPGRADE: Right-click doubler onto backpack

SIZES: 27 or 54 slots

SAVES: Automatic on close
=====================================
PAGE 15
=====================================
END OF GUIDE

Your backpack is ready to use.

Happy adventuring!
